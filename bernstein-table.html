Asheville's climate is a draw for many. Winters bring snowfall levels well below the national average. The average high temperature in January is 48 degrees Fahrenheit, with an average low of 28 degrees Fahrenheit. Though temperatures do drop below freezing, winters are generally mild.

In summer, expect warm, humid conditions. During July, the hottest month of the year, average highs reach 83 degrees. Summer is also the wettest time of year, with a rainy season that lasts from June through September.
